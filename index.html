Hello my name is Margaret Hudak and I am a UX/UI designer.

I specialize in Museum Studies, experiential design, and enjoy solving user navigation problems that limits user accessibility.

My UX Skills

User Empathy

I always focus on the wants and needs of the client, and the exisiting abilities of the user to develop and customize an optimal experience.

Wireframing

I design paper prototypes, low fidelity, and high fidelity wireframes in order to ensure my design meets user criteria and is easily navigational.

Visual Design

I conference with the client and or user about their requests and present a few mock ups to show how to execute their ideas for the final product.
